Our Story

Loco Tranz is an online bus ticket booking platform designed to solve real-world problems faced by daily and long-distance public transportation users.

Many passengers who rely on long-distance MTC buses often face challenges such as uncertainty about seat availability, completely manual ticket booking processes, and inconvenience during daily travel. These issues lead to wasted time and an uncomfortable travel experience for commuters.

Loco Tranz addresses these problems by providing a simple and reliable digital solution. The platform allows users to search for buses, check real-time seat availability, select their preferred seats, and book tickets online with ease. By automating the ticket booking process, Loco Tranz reduces manual effort and improves the overall travel experience.

The system is built with a strong backend that manages seat allocation efficiently, ensuring accurate and real-time seat tracking while preventing double bookings. In addition, Loco Tranz includes an admin panel that helps manage bus schedules, routes, seat availability, and maintenance status.

Loco Tranz aims to make public transportation more accessible, efficient, and user-friendly. In the future, the platform plans to introduce AI-powered features that allow users to book tickets using voice commands or simple text prompts, further enhancing convenience and accessibility.
