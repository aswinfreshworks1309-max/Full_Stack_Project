Our Story

LocoTranz was founded on a simple principle: travel should be as enjoyable as the destination. What started as a small fleet of luxury coaches has grown into India's premier road travel service, connecting people with comfort and class.

We believe in punctuality, safety, and an uncompromising standard of service. Every bus in our fleet is equipped with state-of-the-art amenities to ensure your journey is seamless.
